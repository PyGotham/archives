Ben Leong

Ben Leong is a research engineer at Educational Testing Service (ETS), a non-profit institution performing educational research on assessment quality, validity and fairness. At ETS, Ben helps to develop automated systems for scoring TOEFL, GRE essays and speech. He has also recently began working on the exciting prospect of automated assessment of public speaking proficiency using multimodal cues, such as head gestures, facial expressions and bodily movements. Read more about his work at http://benleong.net
