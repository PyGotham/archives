Ewa Jodlowska

Ewa works at the Python Software Foundation. In 2008 she began working as a third-party meeting planner for PyCon US. In 2011 Ewa left her previous employer and became the Event Coordinator for the PSF. The following year she also picked up the role of PSF Administrator. She now holds the title of Director of Operations and enjoys working with the PSF staff and volunteers.

In her spare time Ewa loves to run (slowly) and to be outdoors with her husband. She is also attempting to learn how to play the violin and is enjoying the long journey (even if everyone else living in her building is not). Most of all, Ewa loves to eat delicious food (hence the need for running). Restaurant & recipe recommendations are always welcome!
