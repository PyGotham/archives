Brian Muller

Brian Muller is the CTO at Wiser, a content recommendation engine for the enterprise. Before that, he held the position of Director of Data Science at a number of companies, including Off Grid Electric (in Tanzania), Vox Media, and LivingSocial. He co-founded the startup OpBandit, a content optimization tool for online publishers, which was acquired by Vox Media in April 2015. Brian has also worked as an adviser and consultant for a variety of groups including the Washington Post, PBS, and the Carnegie Foundation. He has a MS in the Biomedical Sciences and was originally trained as a statistician and computational biologist. His work for the Medical University of South Carolina and Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine was focused on squeezing meaningful information out of vast quantities of genomic data.
