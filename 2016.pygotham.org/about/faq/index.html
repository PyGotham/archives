FAQ

I'm speaking at the conference. Will you cover my travel and lodging costs?

Sorry, no. While we'd love to provide free travel, lodging, and gokarts to everyone that attends the conference, we simply don't have the sponsorship required to do so. Want to change that?

I want to volunteer.

Great! You can sign up from your dashboard. Weâ€™re always looking for session chairs, people to help with A/V, and general runners. We'll reach out to those who've signed up with more details as we get closer to the event. In the mean time, feel free to ping us at volunteers@pygotham.org if you have any questions.

I want to apply for one of PyGotham's scholarships.

Absolutely! We aim for PyGotham to be open to all to attend, and we strive to make the conference as accessible as possible. If you aren't able to obtain a ticket for financial or other reasons, please fill out the application at https://goo.gl/forms/uEQ55S18V5, and we'll arrange for a reduced amount or a free scholarship ticket to ensure that you can attend. Feel free to ping us at scholarships@pygotham.org if you have any questions.

I can only attend part of the conference. Are partial tickets available?

Unfortunately we don't have a way to limit passes to only part of the conference, so only full access tickets are available.

I have another question, and I solemnly swear that it is not answered on this page already.

We're happy to answer it for you. Email us at organizers@pygotham.org.
