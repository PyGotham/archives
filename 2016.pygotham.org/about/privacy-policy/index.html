Privacy Policy

Information we collect
We collect names, email addresses, and passwords. Passwords are encrypted, and we have no way of retrieving them. If your password is lost, you must reset it through our forgot password page.
What we do with this information
We take privacy very seriously. We use your contact information to send updates and conference related info to attendees. Information may be shared with third-party vendors conducting business on behalf of the conference. It is not used for any other purpose, nor is it given or sold to any other party, including other attendees or sponsors.

Feel free to contact organizers@pygotham.org for details involving privacy.
