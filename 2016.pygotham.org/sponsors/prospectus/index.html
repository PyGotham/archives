So you want to sponsor PyGotham 2016

You're awesome!

PyGotham 2016 is New York City's largest gathering of Python developers. Brought to you by the same people who organize NYC Python, Flask-NYC, and Learn Python NYC, PyGotham 2016 draws a diverse crowd with backgrounds in many different industries.

In order to keep the registration costs as low as possible, we need your help. Several different levels of sponsorship are available. If you have any questions, you can contact us directly.

Once you're ready, go ahead and send us an email.

Contact

sponsors@pygotham.org

Sponsorship Levels

Diamond ($15,000)
You will receive:
  • a sponsor talk (subject to final approval)
  • your logo on a bumper for the conference videos
  • your logo on a banner at the conference
  • your logo displayed on the sponsors page
  • your logo in media about and at the conference
  • 10 tickets
  • 15 registration scholarships given on your behalf
Platinum Sponsor ($8,000)
You will receive:
  • a booth near the registration area
  • your logo on a banner at the conference
  • your logo displayed on the sponsors page
  • your logo in media about and at the conference
  • 6 tickets
  • 10 registration scholarships given on your behalf
Gold Sponsor ($5,000)
You will receive:
  • a small booth in the lounge area
  • your logo displayed on the sponsors page
  • your logo in media about and at the conference
  • 4 tickets
  • 5 registration scholarships given on your behalf
Silver Sponsor ($3,000)
You will receive:
  • your logo displayed on the sponsors page
  • your logo in media about and at the conference
  • 3 tickets
Media Sponsor (N/A)
You will receive:
  • your logo displayed on the sponsors page
  • your logo in media about and at the conference
