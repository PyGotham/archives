Joseph Mosby

Joseph Mosby is a web developer at the National Journal, an American politics and policy magazine in Washington, D.C. His team is currently redesigning and rebuilding National Journal's online publishing platform, converting a legacy PHP system into a fully-featured Django project. He writes regularly about obscure Python topics, web development, and data analysis.
