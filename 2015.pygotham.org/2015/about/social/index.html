The Unnamed PyGotham Social Event

By popular demand, the PyGotham social event returns! What better way to end the first day of PyGotham 2015 than with an evening of socializing and refreshments? Come join us at Bitly HQ for a chance to hang out and talk about your favorite parts of PyGotham, or anything else you want to share.

The Unnamed PyGotham Social Event is brought to you by Bitly, SinglePlatform, and iHeartRadio. Attendance is free with your PyGotham 2015 ticket.
