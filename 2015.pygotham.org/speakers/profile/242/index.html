Christine Doig

Data Scientist at Continuum Analytics. M.S. in Industrial Engineering from UPC, Barcelona.

After graduate school, Christine worked in applied Operations Research at Procter&Gamble. As a Process Engineer and Project Manager, she was in charge of analyzing and optimizing manufacturing processes. Before joining Continuum, she worked as a Business Analyst and Consultant for a management consulting firm in the finance sector.

Christine has a multinational background, has lived in three different countries and is fluent in four languages including German and Spanish. When she is not writing python code, she loves to travel. She is an active member in the Python community, participates in several meetups in Austin and is a regular speaker at conferences including PyData, PyCon and SciPy.
