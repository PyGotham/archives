What's Included?

Along with your conference registration, attendees will receive breakfast, lunch, and break refreshments.

Refund Policy

Refunds requested more than 48 hours prior to the start of the event will be processed, resulting in a return of the full purchase price of the ticket. (Corporate sponsors who include an additional donation, please specify if you wish to have the donation returned as well.)

We apologize that within 48 hours of the event, catering and capacity numbers will be locked in. We will not be able to provide a refund at that time.
