So you want to sponsor PyGotham 2014

You're awesome!

PyGotham 2014 is New York City's largest gathering of Python developers. Brought to you by the same people who organize NYC Python and Flask-NYC, PyGotham 2014 draws a diverse crowd with backgrounds in many different industries.

In order to keep the registration costs as low as possible, we need your help. Several different levels of sponsorship are available. If you have any questions, you can contact us directly.

Contact

sponsors@pygotham.org

Sponsorship Levels

Diamond Sponsor ($15,000)
You will receive:
  • a sponsored room for one day, program subject to approval
  • your logo on a banner at the conference
  • your logo displayed on the sponsors page
  • your logo in media about and at the conference
  • 10 tickets
  • 15 registration scholarships given on your behalf
Platinum Sponsor ($7,500)
You will receive:
  • a booth near the registration area
  • your logo on a banner at the conference
  • your logo displayed on the sponsors page
  • your logo in media about and at the conference
  • 7 tickets
  • 10 registration scholarships given on your behalf
Gold Sponsor ($5,000) sold out
You will receive:

  • your logo in media about and at the conference
  • 5 tickets
  • 5 registration scholarships given on your behalf
