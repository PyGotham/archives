FAQ

I'm speaking at the conference. Will you cover my travel and lodging costs?

Sorry, no. While we'd love to provide free travel, lodging, and gokarts to everyone that attends the conference, we simply don't have the sponsorship required to do so. Want to change that?

I missed the CFP deadline, can I submit a talk late?

We're already full-up on talks, but keep it handy for next year.

I want to volunteer.

Great! Fill out the form, and we'll be in touch soon. Feel free to ping us at this address volunteers@pygotham.org, if you have any questions.

I have another question, and I solemnly swear that it is not answered on this page already.

We're happy to answer it for you. Email us at organizers@pygotham.org.

I want to apply for one of PyGotham's scholarships.

Absolutely! Just fill out the form, and we will be in touch by August 11th if your application has been accepted to receive a scholarship. Feel free to ping us at this address scholarships@pygotham.org, if you have any questions.
